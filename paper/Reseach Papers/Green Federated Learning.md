# Paper Summary 

## Paper Title: Green Federated Learning
## Authors: Amir Yousefpour et al.
## Year: 2023

**Full Citation:**Yousefpour, A., Guo, S., Shenoy, A., et al. (2023). Green Federated Learning. arXiv preprint arXiv:2303.14604.https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2303.14604
## 1. Problem
Even federated learning may result in a high total energy consumption across a wide range of devices.

## 2. Approach
The authors provide measured results for the carbon footprint and propose a carbon-aware approach.

## 3. Key Findings
Distributed AI systems reduce the load on the servers but result in high energy consumption for the devices.

## 4. Relevance to Our Topic
This strongly supports the energy-hungry AI side with real measured data.
